    • About Me

    Hello, I'm Jaeden Cross and the first thing I'd like to tell you about me is that I'm not some extremely confident, extroverted, over-achiever. I'm not a natural born leader, or an out-going grassroot that can connect everyone and bring the world together. I am a anti-nihilistic person that wants to cultivate their creativity and use that as a medium to reach out to others. I love to experience new things and have a deep appreciation for nature. I'd love to travel and experience all the world has to offer while helping and learning more about the wild-life we've become thoroughly disconnected from.
